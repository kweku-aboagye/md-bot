# Music Director

I am the music director over the music ministry for ICGC Praise Temple ([https://www.icgcpraisetempletx.org/](https://www.icgcpraisetempletx.org/)), a local branch of a Ghanaian-based charismatic church ICGC ([https://centralgospel.com/](https://centralgospel.com/)). I have been serving in this position since 2024.

The music ministry comprises of multiple sub groups:

- Praise and Worship Team: They handle the worship and praise sessions during each Sunday Service
- Celestial Choir: They are tasked to minister a hymn each Sunday service
- His Glory Heralds: This is a youth ministration team which ministers a song each Sunday service
- Zamar band: This is the team of instrumentalists that support all the above teams with music. There is a youth version of the team of instrumentalists called Youth Zamar Band

## Leadership

This is the structure of leadership of the music ministry:

- Music ministry Pastor
- Music Director (myself)
- Assistant Music Director

For each sub group in the ministry (execept the Zamar band), they have the following leaders:

- President
- Organizing secretary

The Zamar band is directed by the music director and the assistant music director

## Constitutionary Role of Music Director

The Music Director (MD) serves as the primary musical leader for the Praise & Worship Team, Celestial Choir, His Glory Heralds, and is responsible for: 

- Directing rehearsals, ministrations, and musical activities of the ministry. 
- Infusing spiritual depth into musical selections and interpretations, aligning them with the church's events, teachings, and values. 
- Providing guidance and musical instruction to members. 
- Collaborating with the church leadership and other committees for planning music-related activities.

## Week in the life of a music director

In this section I break down what i do for each of the groups

### Celestial Choir

Celestial Choir is more free flowing, they are an older section of the ministry and thus I mostly give them the leeway to select their own hymn and minister. Typically here is my role:

- I have delegated the song selection to the Assistant MD, President and Secretary to come up with the song
- I created this sheet ([https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZfPTMRu8Z8CbOAjk553jq-O5vtywZ5_8jlU6GNQuaDA/edit?gid=1798374687#gid=1798374687](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZfPTMRu8Z8CbOAjk553jq-O5vtywZ5_8jlU6GNQuaDA/edit?gid=1798374687#gid=1798374687)) for them to upload their chosen hymn there
- This team rehearses Sunday after service, the hymn they are going to minister the following Sunday

### Praise and Worship

This is one of the core teams in the ministry to me, given that praise and worship is the most integral part of the ministry. Here is their flow

- This is the document where we track the song selections ([https://docs.google.com/document/d/1SD2t9J7jYZUnN9QDOr2TWgtfkEkOfe4yuxYYb1_WwLY/edit?tab=t.0](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1SD2t9J7jYZUnN9QDOr2TWgtfkEkOfe4yuxYYb1_WwLY/edit?tab=t.0))

- This team rehearses on Sunday after the Celestial Choir rehearsal for the songs they are going to minister the following Sunday
- This team also ministers during our monthly Half night service (First Friday of the month at 9PM)

### Zamar Band

As a keyboard player myself, this is an integral and my favorite team. The band has the hardest task to prepare all the music to support all the other ministries songs. Here is their flow

- This team rehearses Wednesday nights, but the difference is they rehearse the music for the Sunday rehearsal. So essentially they rehearse a week ahead, to prepare the music. For example, if this team meets this Wednesday, they are preparing for this week's rehearsal which is for the next week Sunday's actual service
- During offering sessions for Sunday (we have two of them), the band plays a very nice and vibrant song as the congregation pays offering which we rehearse. We choose the songs on the fly, mostly inspired by other bands on Youtube, Tiktok etc
- We also come up with innovative intros, transition and outro tunes for all the music that we are given
- The Youth band, given they are in the prep stage are mostly given some of the offering sessions in the month to take over, or they also take over some services like the Youth Service, Children service e.t.c
- This team doesnt really do documents, i have tried before ([https://docs.google.com/document/d/1bKBxGl7Xc5oy0tsubTGdkszbkzbpFTyzkBOTo-3kIUw/edit?tab=t.j6m1rlrvms1s](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1bKBxGl7Xc5oy0tsubTGdkszbkzbpFTyzkBOTo-3kIUw/edit?tab=t.j6m1rlrvms1s))

For all the groups i also:

- Facilitate communication: Ensure that everyone is aware of everything going on with the help of the leaders. We use whatsapp as our primary communication platform
- Teach vocal work. I try to teach harmonies using my knowledge from playing the piano to enhance the singing
- Ensure we always have enough people for rehearsals and Sunday service, if not I have to outsource from congregation for help if we need
- If we are missing folks in the band, I have to coordinate help from outside the church
- I go to the extent of having to pickup and dropoff some members (especially the youth) for rehearsals, some church services etc
